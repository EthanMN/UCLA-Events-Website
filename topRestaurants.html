Here are 5 good restaurants in Westwood

Bruin Bowl Malatang

Build-Your-Own soup style restaurant that also serves sweet and savory appetizers

Mandi House

Yemeni restaurant serving breakfast, seafood, and authentic dishes such as yemeni asida

Hangry Moon's Fried Chicken

Fried chicken spot that serves a variety of fried chicken dishes and bowls

Wolfsglen

Fine dining restaurant with wine bar that serves dishes ranging from steak to pasta and salmon

Curry Hero

Restaurant specializing in curry rice dishes but also has appetizers and salads with deserts like mochi as well

Location: 1064 Gayley Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90024
