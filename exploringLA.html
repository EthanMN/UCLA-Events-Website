Exploring Los Angeles can be a great way to take a break from classes and have fun with friends! Here are some of the best spots to explore in LA!

Griffith Observatory is a famous landmark in Los Angeles with stunning views of the city. It's a great place to visit and learn about astronomy and space exploration.

Santa Monica Pier is a beautiful beachfront with roller coasters and carnival games. It's a great place to enjoy the ocean breeze and have fun with friends.

Universal Studios Hollywood is a famous theme park with exciting attractions and entertainment. There’s an endless amount of things to do for an unforgettable experience.

Westfield Century City is located just a few miles from UCLA. It offers a variety of stores and entertainment options perfect for shopping and relaxation.

Koreatown is a vibrant neighborhood with amazing restaurants, shops, and nightlife. It's a great place to experience Korean culture and cuisine.

The Getty Center is a world-famous art museum with incredible exhibits and architecture. You are sure to be amazed when you visit.
