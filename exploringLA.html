Exploring Los Angeles can be a great way to take a break from classes and have fun with friends! Here are some of the best spots to explore in LA!

Griffith Observatory is a famous landmark in Los Angeles with stunning views of the city. It's a great place to visit and learn about astronomy and space exploration.

Santa Monica Pier is a beautiful beachfront and has a variety of attractions such as roller coasters and carnival games. It's a great place to visit and enjoy the ocean breeze.

Universal Studios Hollywood is a famous theme park in Los Angeles with a variety of attractions and entertainment. There is an endless amount of things to do and will surely provide an unforgettable experience for visitors.

Westfield is in the heart of Century City just a few miles away from UCLA! It has a variety of stores and entertainment options which are perfect for shopping and relaxation.

Koreatown is a vibrant neighborhood in Los Angeles with a variety of restaurants, shops, and entertainment options. It's a great place to visit and experience Korean culture and cuisine.

The Getty Center is a famous art museum in Los Angeles with a variety of exhibits and collections. You are sure to be in awe once you visit!
